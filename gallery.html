What is Portrait Photography

Portrait photography is the type of photography where photographer focuses on capturing the client's facial features and with it his/her attitude, identity and personality. Depending on the client needs, whether that he needs a photo for job application or social media, the photographer will carefully plan out and craft the environment in which he will take photos of the client.
